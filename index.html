Latinamerican multiaccent Text-to-Speech Services

About Neurasound

We are a group of audio an machine learning reaserchers based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Our main goal is to bring AI technology to latinamerican markets, pushing the limits of voice synthesis.

For more information contact us: info@neurasound.com.ar

 

Product description

Nowadays, very few spanish TTS exist in the market, and most of them have a neutral, Spain or Mexican accent. We focused our efforts in providing an argentinian spanish TTS, which feels more natural to argentinian users and constitutes the main novelty of our project. We also experimented with accents from other latinamerican countries like Colombia and Chile, by curating a multi-accent spanish dataset.

Main features

Our model supports a variety of latinamerican spanish accents (Argentina, Chile, Colombia, España, México, Perú, Puerto Rico, Uruguay and Venezuela)

Voice cloning: By performing speaker adaptation with less than 10 minutes of speech from the target speaker.

Prosody control: Duration, pitch and energy of the synthetic voice can be controlled.
